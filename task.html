The Pomodoro Break Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a simple way to stay focused and avoid burnout.
-Work for 25 minutes on a single task with no distractions.
-Take a 5-minute break to relax, stretch, or grab water.
-After four cycles, enjoy a longer break of 15–30 minutes.
This rhythm of focused work and regular breaks helps boost concentration, energy, and productivity.
